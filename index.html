About me

I consider myself a skilled person who easily adapts to different situations and working conditions.

I am interested in studying coherent structures that emerge from nonlinear models of continuous media. My recent work includes research on phase transitions in complex materials, the spectral and nonlinear stability of magnetic domains in ferromagnetic thin films, and the spectral stability of gas diffusion in glassy polymers. I am a proudly "Hecho en México" mathematician (with a physicists background) whose math-research interests include:

  • Calculus of Variations
  • Spectral and Nonlinear Stability of Coherent Structures
  • Partial Differential Equations
  • Analysis
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Scientific Programming

I really enjoy treking with my wife and pulling espressos.
