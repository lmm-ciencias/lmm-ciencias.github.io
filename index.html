About me

I consider myself a skilled person who easily adapts to different situations and working conditions.

I am interested in the study of complex material phenomena such as phase transitions, nucleation,  and stability and dynamics of microstructure.  I am a proudly "Hecho en México" mathematician (with a physicists background) whose math-research interests include:

  • Calculus of Variations
  • Partial Differential Equations
  • Analysis
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Scientific Programming

I really enjoy treking with my wife and pulling espressos.
